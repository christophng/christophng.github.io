WEBSITE DESIGN

A must for any business, websites offer an easy-to-access area in which all of your important information can be placed. A beautiful and unique yet functional website seperates you from the crowd and creates a great first impression on potential customers.

PROCESS

  • 1. CONSULTATION

    We ask for your needs, wants, and desired look. Meanwhile, our team of designers assess your brand for colors, themes, and moods in order to create an idea of a great style that fits your business. 

  • 2. PROTOTYPING

    Our team starts to design and create the first prototypes, which are sent to you for confirmation before starting. Feel free to suggest changes and features to add.

  • 3. DEVELOPING

    We start to plug in all the details and code into the functioning product, with your input along the way.

  • 4. FINAL DRAFT

    The final design is sent to you for a final look, where any last minute suggestions and changes can be made.

  • 5. VOILA!

    We upload or send you the web files  for your personally designed, beautiful website! Enjoy!
